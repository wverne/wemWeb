This is web interface for wem (Wesley's Exoplanet Modeler), a set of programs for modeling the properties of solid exoplanets.

wem consists of three executables, two of which can be run using this web interface:

  • wemEP: Exoplanet Profiler

    Generates profiles of individual exoplanets, consisting of pressure, density, and interior mass with respect to radius. A core equation of state (EOS) must be given and any number of additional EOS layers (specified by the mass fraction at which they begin) may be added.

  • wemMR: Mass-Radius Plot Generator

    Generates mass-radius plots for a given composition, consisting of radius, central pressure, central density, energy data (gravitational and mechanical), and moment of inertia coefficient. The same equation of state specifications as in wemEP may be made, and a mass range and interval may be specified.
